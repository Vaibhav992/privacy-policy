Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Instagram Automation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Instagram automation services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Instagram Account Information: When you connect your Instagram account, we collect your Instagram username, profile information, and account ID.
  • Access Tokens: We securely store Instagram access tokens to provide automation services such as automated responses to comments and messages.
  • Automation Rules: We store your automation rules and preferences that you configure in our service.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our service, including automation execution counts and interaction logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Instagram automation services
  • Process and respond to comments and messages on your Instagram account
  • Execute automation rules as configured by you
  • Improve and optimize our services
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely in our database and access tokens are encrypted. We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With your explicit consent

6. Instagram API Usage

Our service uses Instagram's Graph API and Basic Display API. By using our service, you agree to Instagram's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy. We only access the minimum required permissions to provide our automation services.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your Instagram account at any time
  • Modify or delete your automation rules

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting your Instagram account or contacting us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
